New beginnings

posted on 29/09/2017





I can’t believe this month marks our one year anniversary of living in our first family home! What a crazy busy
and beautiful year it has been! I have just finished my degree (I am actually working on my first webdesign project
for a company right now!) and Luna has started preschool this month. Thinking back to last year when we had just
moved, we didn’t have that many friends here, and I had just started studying again.

One year later and I have a couple of beautiful friendships that I cherish so much, and I am working from home now, something that has been a major goal for me ever since I became a single mom.





All of our stuff, packed up in 12 boxes!





One of the things I fell in love with pretty soon after we moved here, was the light in this house! Especially the soft
winter light is just beautiful.
